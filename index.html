Table Tennis Passion

Welcome to the Exciting World of Table Tennis

Table Tennis, also known as Ping-Pong, is a fast-paced, exciting sport that combines quick reflexes, strategy, and skill. It's played on a table divided by a net, with players using small paddles to hit a lightweight ball back and forth.

Why Play Table Tennis?

  • Improves hand-eye coordination
  • Enhances reflexes and mental acuity
  • Low-impact exercise suitable for all ages
  • Develops strategic thinking
  • Fun for both casual and competitive players

Equipment Needed

  • Table Tennis table
  • Paddles (also called rackets)
  • Table Tennis balls
  • Comfortable sports attire

Basic Rules

  1. Serve the ball so it bounces once on your side, then once on the opponent's side
  2. Players alternate hitting the ball over the net
  3. Points are scored when your opponent fails to return the ball to your side
  4. Games are typically played to 11 points, with players serving two points each
